Teaching

I teach courses on comparative politics and quantitative methods. At Harvard, I have been a teaching fellow for the following courses:

  • Foundations of Comparative Politics (GOV 20) — Fall 2024
  • Democracy and Authoritarianism (GOV 1290) — Spring 2025
  • Elections and Representation in the Developing World (GOV 1146) — Fall 2025

In the Spring semester of 2026, I am serving as lead instructor for GOV 97 — Parties and Campaigns in the Digital Age, a sophomore tutorial.

I also have experience advising senior theses in comparative politics. If you are a Gov undergrad looking for an advisor, feel free to shoot me an email and I would be happy to chat.

If you are a student currently taking a class I am teaching and want to book an office hours appointment, you can do so on Calendly.
